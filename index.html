WITCHCRAFT IN SCOTLAND

by madeline everett

Madeline's Website
When I was studying at Oxford, I took a course called 'Magic in the Middle Ages'. It sounds super cool, right? And that must be what this project is about, then. Well, not exactly. The truth is that the witch hunts started after middle ages, with the start of the protestant reformation (1517). In the map you can see the growth of the persecution of witches around Scotland. The graph on the right shows one area in particular, the Lothians, where persecution was especially heavy.
